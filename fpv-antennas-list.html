The complete list of FPV antennas

This is the list of antennas currently supported by maxmyrange.com, the definitive FPV range calculator.
If you spot anything missing or out of date please let me know!

A few notes:
  • Gain values for different antennas were taken from manufacturer published information, as shown below.
  • I don't endorse any particular antenna manufacturer so I've taken the photos at random from a selection of vendors
  • The "common frequencies" column shows which frequencies each antenna can commonly be found in in FPV stores. In theory you could build most antenna designs at most frequencies
  • Where 1.3 is stated its most likely that 1.2 can also be found. Same with 2.4 and 2.3
