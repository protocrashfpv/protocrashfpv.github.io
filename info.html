Maximise your FPV range

MaxMyRange calculates your total theoretical range based on your setup's frequency, power and antennas.


The result is the theoretical best case range of your system. Real life range will depend on a number of additional factors. As such, please only use these calculations as indicative and always fly well within your theoretical range. And don't forget, range is not the only factor when building an FPV setup - excessive power can cause interference, low frequency results in larger antennas and each antenna's radiation pattern is different.

Please feel free to send any and all feedback on how to improve the site and requests for changes or additions to the assumptions used
